\section{Objectives of Research}
The objectives of this research are as follows.

\begin{enumerate}[label=\arabic*.,leftmargin=2em,itemsep=2pt,topsep=0pt]
  \item To design a shared-backbone multi-task architecture for cattle detection, individual re-identification, and behaviour recognition.
  \item To develop a masked-label heterogeneous training scheme enabling joint optimisation across datasets with incomplete label sets.
  \item To integrate tracking as a downstream association step that reuses the learned re-identification embeddings.
  \item To evaluate the framework against single-task and pipeline baselines on public cattle datasets.
\end{enumerate}
